About Me

Hello, Fotis here! I'm a Copenhagen-based Product designer & HCI specialist, with a developer's touch.
My job is to navigate the intersection or user needs, business goals and technical constraints, by leading the entire design process and ensure all aspects of a product align, providing great user experiences.
My background is quite diverse. I have an MSc in Engineering, in the field of Human-centered AI and prior to that I had worked as a UI developer, after finishing my BSc in Computer Science. But before all that, I was a joint CEO of a commercial-technical company, with two production units of expanded polystyrene.
These experiences have shaped my career and give me the advantage of being able to understand in depth the needs of a project.
